WELCOME TO BeWUST

BeWust foods History

“ Bewust is a Dutch word means Conscious, signifying transparency & authenticity of our work. Practicing dietetics and nutrition research, three friends living in 3 different cities faced one problem. Dearth of safe ready-to-eat food. For recommending their clients or for own consumption, they could not find a single brand which is delicious yet totally healthy, real & affordable. This search led to an idea & thus their love for Nutrition, Agriculture & Food evolved as Bewust ”

Tannu Shree Singh - Founder BEWUST Foods
